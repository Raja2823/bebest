Services we provide for Canada PR aspirants:

Express Entry Programs

    Eligibility for Express Entry programs

    Canada chooses skilled workers based on their skills and ability to contribute to Canada’s economy. Express Entry manages applications for three economic immigration programs.

  • Federal Skilled Worker Program

    This program is for skilled workers with foreign work experience who want to immigrate to Canada permanently. It requires minimum one year of skilled work experience in Managerial, professional or technical jobs and skilled traders with high language proficiency in English or French.

  • Federal Skilled Trades Program

    The Federal Skilled Trades Program is for skilled workers who want to become permanent residents based on being qualified in a skilled trade. It requires minimum two years of experience in occupations that falls under Federal Skilled Trades Program with good language proficiency in English or French.

  • Canadian Experience Class

    The Canadian Experience Class is for skilled workers who have Canadian work experience and want to become permanent residents. This program requires one year of skilled work experience in Canada with good language proficiency in English or French

  • Provincial Nominee Program (PNP)

    PNP is a program intended for the regions of Canada with the goal that they designate and select especially talented laborers who satisfy their necessities. The candidate that is welcome to apply should then live and work in the region which named and supported them. The program is overseen by the regions and just uses the Express Entry portal to get profiles on the online.

  • Visit Visa:

    Visitor visas are nonimmigrant visas for persons who want to enter the Canada temporarily for business, for tourism, or for a combination of both purposes.

  • Dependent Visa:

    Dependent visa allows you to invite your immediate family members to migrate to Canada and it also allows them to work or study full time.
