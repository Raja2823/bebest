Our Services

Career Guidance (Choosing the right University/Course)

At Be Best, student profiles are thoroughly vetted and assessed to provide the student with recommendations that would help them to achieve their desired career outcomes. Be Best career advisors assist you in narrowing university choices based on the chosen career stream and in consonance with various other constraints. Be Best career advisors are qualified resources of information and are trained to guide you through the selection of courses, institutions and destinations.

Organising Educational fairs and seminars

​ Be Best conducts several awareness events annually for the benefit of the student community, including, educational fairs and seminars, so that visiting delegates from representative universities and experienced overseas education advisors could dispense information regarding the courses and other areas pertaining to overseas education. These events also provide an opportunity for students to apply for admissions to the visiting universities or avail of any special offers or discounted application fees.

Assistance with University applications

To ensure that your candidature stands out amongst the numerous applications that Universities receive, Be Best offers guidance and support required to understand the tedious process of applications and a better understanding of the expectations of the university and the chosen program of study. Be Best services extend to providing guidelines to frame an impressive Statement of Purpose along with emphasizing a candidate’s individual strengths.

Language Exam Coaching

Be Best, together with our affiliate; ACCT, provide test preparation training for: IELTS, TOEFL, PTE, GRE, SAT, and GMAT test formats. Our career advisors ensure that they meticulously follow admission trends at various universities, keep tabs on current trends, changes to immigration rules, policies of destination countries, and keep track of employability trends, with the intention of advising and guiding you with the most relevant and up-to-date information.

Post- selection assistance (Applying for a student visa)

Our services do not end with candidates securing a place in the University. Be Best education advisors are well versed with the visa application requirements of different countries and provide assistance in securing the required visa for the duration of your studies, facilitate foreign exchange and remittance of fees, and conduct pre-departure orientations on the destination country, including information about the country’s climate, accommodation, living costs, travel, and more, to enable you to have a smooth departure and a comfortable acclimatisation upon landing at your destination.

Post arrival assistance

Be Best also offers post arrival assistance to the students that we place in various universities across the world, including airport pickups and finding suitable temporary accommodation for students, in collaboration with respective universities.
